Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with GreenBite? +
Getting started is easy! Simply sign up on our website, complete your health profile, and our system will generate personalized recommendations for workouts, nutrition plans, and mindfulness practices based on your goals.
Do I need any special equipment for the workouts? +
Most of our workouts are designed to be done with minimal equipment. Some routines may recommend dumbbells or resistance bands, but we always provide alternative exercises that use only body weight.
Can I customize my meal plans? +
Absolutely! Our nutrition system allows you to set dietary preferences, exclude specific ingredients, and adjust portion sizes based on your caloric needs and goals.
How often is new content added? +
We add new workouts, recipes, and mindfulness exercises every week. Subscribers also get early access to new features and exclusive content.
What if I have specific health concerns? +
While our platform provides general wellness guidance, we always recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before starting any new fitness or nutrition program, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions.
